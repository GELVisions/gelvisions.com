About GEL Visions

GEL Visions addresses two critical global challenges simultaneously: plastic pollution and agricultural stubble burning.

By collecting discarded plastic and agricultural stubble, we're able to manufacture durable, eco-friendly bricks that are used to build safe, affordable housing for those in need.

Our innovative approach not only provides sustainable building materials but also creates local employment opportunities and reduces environmental pollution.

How It Works

The Problem We're Solving

Plastic pollution and stubble burning are creating environmental and health crises worldwide.

Plastic Pollution

Millions of tons of plastic waste end up in landfills and oceans each year, harming wildlife and ecosystems. Only 9% of all plastic ever produced has been recycled.

Stubble Burning

Farmers burn agricultural residue after harvest, releasing toxic pollutants that cause severe air pollution and respiratory problems for millions of people.

Housing Shortage

Over 1 billion people live in inadequate housing, with limited access to affordable building materials. Traditional brick production consumes valuable topsoil.

How Collection Works

After you submit the donation form:

1. Our team will review your submission within 24 hours

2. A driver will call you to confirm details and schedule pickup

3. We collect waste twice per week - typically Wednesdays and Saturdays

4. You'll receive a confirmation call the day before collection

5. Our truck will pick up your waste at the scheduled time
